About Coast Phlebotomy

Our Commitment

To provide the best care, period. Our staff is a family of compassionate professionals here to help you.

After discovering the unmet needs of those that cannot make it to our lab, and collaborating with Health-care organizations we launched our mobile phlebotomy service.

We come to you and take care of the lab work as well. Our team of licensed professionals, provide diagnostic and other concierge services in the comfort of your home or business.

We provide phlebotomy services to all major hospitals, clinics, integrative practices, businesses, individuals, Law Enforcement, the Military and The Red Cross. We provide timely, accurate services that are dependable.
